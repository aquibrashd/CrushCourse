Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025  ·  Effective: April 2025
Your privacy matters to us. CrushCourse does not sell your data, does not create user accounts, and does not store your personal information on our servers. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

1. Who We Are

CrushCourse ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered relationship coaching platform available at crushcourse.xyz. We help users with love strategy, smart replies, compatibility insights, and gift ideas.

For questions about this policy, contact us at: crushcourse.xyz@gmail.com

2. Information You Provide

When you use CrushCourse, you may enter information such as:

  • Names, ages, genders, and personality descriptions of you and your crush
  • Birthday dates (used only to calculate zodiac signs and age)
  • Messages you receive (for Smart Reply suggestions)
  • Social media profile links (optional, used only to improve AI analysis)
  • Occasion and budget details (for Gift Ideas)

This information is sent to our AI service to generate responses and is not stored on our servers. It is processed in real-time and discarded after your response is generated.

3. Data We Do NOT Store

CrushCourse does not:

  • Create user accounts or require login
  • Store your personal details, messages, or inputs on our servers
  • Save your compatibility results or love strategy outputs
  • Access, store, or share your social media data
  • Sell your data to third parties
Social media links you share are sent directly to the AI for analysis and are never stored, logged, or shared. We cannot access your private profiles — only publicly available information is used.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq AI — processes your inputs to generate AI responses. Groq's privacy policy applies to data sent to their servers. No personally identifiable information is included beyond what you type.
  • Google Analytics — collects anonymised usage data (pages visited, time on site, device type, country). This helps us understand how CrushCourse is used. No personal details are shared with Google Analytics.
  • Netlify — hosts our website and serverless functions. Standard server logs may include IP addresses for security purposes.

5. Cookies

CrushCourse uses minimal browser storage:

  • localStorage — saves your theme preference (dark/light mode, colour) so the site looks the same when you return.
  • sessionStorage — temporarily saves form inputs within your browser tab so you don't lose your work if you accidentally close something. This data is cleared when you close your tab.
  • Google Analytics cookies — used to track anonymous usage statistics. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

6. Children's Privacy

CrushCourse is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take immediate steps to address it.
